# What Derivity Is Not

## What Derivity Is Not

### Purpose

This page defines explicit boundaries to prevent misuse or misunderstanding of Derivity.

### Non-Goals

Derivity is not:

* a trading bot
* a financial advisor
* a signal or Indicator-generation system
* a general-purpose chatbot
* an automation system that acts on behalf of users

### Why These Boundaries Exist

Financial decisions carry real-world risk.

Systems that overstep their role:

* create over-reliance
* hide uncertainty&#x20;
* increase user harm

Derivity enforces strict boundaries to maintain safety and trust.
